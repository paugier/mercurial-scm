Mercurial is being used by all kinds of companies and projects. On this page we only list 8 picks. For a longer list, please visit the dedicated wiki-page.

Companies

  • Google for internal use and their free project hosting.
    Mercurial offers an excellent "sweet spot" in terms of flexibility, simplicity, and speed. - Alex Martelli, Google Tech Lead.
  • Mozilla for all kinds of projects, including Firefox from 3.x onward.
  • Sun for projects including OpenJDK and Open Solaris.
  • Bitbucket to provide free project hosting.

Community Projects

  • Python for future development.
    It's hard to explain my reasons for choosing -- like most language decisions (especially the difficult ones) it's mostly a matter of gut feelings - Guido van Rossum, Python BDFL
  • Xen for developing their Paravirtualization System.
  • MoinMoin for developing their wiki engine.
  • Adium, for writing their MacOSX Chat client.
